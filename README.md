# Accounting Classroom

## Empowering Accounting Learners and Educators

Accounting Classroom is a modern educational platform designed to support both Accounting learners and Accounting educators through simplified learning resources, professional teaching tools, and assessment support.

The platform serves:
- Grade 10–12 Accounting learners
- First-year Financial Accounting students
- Second-year Financial Accounting students
- Accounting educators and facilitators
- Tutors and academic support providers

---

# Vision

To make Accounting simple, practical, accessible, and engaging for every learner while equipping educators with high-quality academic support tools.

---

# Services Offered

## For High School Accounting Learners (Grade 10–12)

- Simplified Accounting notes
- Exam preparation resources
- Practice activities and quizzes
- Video explanations
- Step-by-step calculations
- Memorandums and worked solutions
- Mobile-friendly learning support

---

## For University Financial Accounting Students

### First-Year Modules
- FAC1601
- FAC1502
- Financial Accounting foundations
- Journals and ledgers
- Statements of Financial Position
- Cash Flow Statements
- Adjustments and reconciliations

### Second-Year Support
- Intermediate Financial Accounting
- Financial Statements
- Interpretation and analysis
- Advanced adjustments
- Accounting calculations and exam preparation

---
